Graphic hearts stack and scatter in shades of orange and red, landing squarely between pop art and cozy nostalgia. Negative space keeps the arrangement breathable, so the intensity feels intentional rather than loud. Pair with neutral upholstery or natural wood for an inviting, sociable tone.
